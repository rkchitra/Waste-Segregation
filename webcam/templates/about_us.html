About Us

We are final year students from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The National Institute of Engineering, Mysore. We aim to help the coporation in automating the process of waste segregation by building a prototype that is able to identify a waste material as biodegradable or non-biodegradable efficiently and push it into the appropriate bin accordingly. Our site has an additional functionality where you can upload images and get them classified. Learn more about the Model

Ragpickers collect recyclables from dump yards and sustain themselves by collecting, sorting and segregating waste and then trading it. They help clean up a significant proportion of the 62 million tonnes of waste generated annually in India. Currently, Rag pickers have to do this task manually, leading to the birth of many diseases due to the unhygenic working conditions. We strive to eliminate this procedure by automating the process.
